Hello! My name is Alex Couture-Beil and welcome to my online portfolio. I live in Vancouver BC and like to take photos for fun. That's it.

I am a hobbyist photographer who likes to capture moments, tell stories, and ask questions with photographs. My first real experience with photography was in highschool where I spent many hours in the dark-room.

After a hiatus, and moving to Vancouver, I learned of street photography and combined this with my passion of unicycling. I started a blog to capture the reactions of people I encountered while unicycling. This project reignited my passion for photography and now I'm looking forward to becoming a better photographer and taking more interesting pictures.

Aside from photography, you can find me writing software, unicycling, brewing beer, kayaking, and enjoying the outdoors.
